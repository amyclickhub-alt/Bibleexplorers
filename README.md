Bible Explorers — 365 Days of God's Word for Kids
A faith-based interactive Bible devotional app for children aged 5–14, built with React and powered by the Anthropic Claude AI API.

✨ Features

365 Daily Lessons — Structured devotionals across 12 Biblical themes (Faith, Obedience, Honour, Kindness, Grace, Mercy, Growth, Wisdom, Courage, Gratitude, Prayer, Love)
5-Step Lesson Flow — Verse → Lesson → Activity → Quiz → Prayer
AI-Powered Prayer Corner — Children write prayers and receive warm, Bible-based AI responses
🔊 Audio Narration — Text-to-speech read-aloud for every lesson and prayer
🏆 Gamification — Star rewards, streaks, and completion tracking
🐾 Mascot Companions — Leo, Dove, Lamb & Eagle guide kids through the journey
👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Dashboard — PIN-protected progress tracker with theme analytics
💳 Subscription Plans — 6-month and 12-month premium tiers (USD & NGN pricing)
📱 Mobile-Friendly — Optimised for phones and tablets
